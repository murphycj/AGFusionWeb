What is AGFusion?

Annotate Gene Fusion (AGFusion) and its accompanying python package predict the cDNA, CDS, and protein
sequences as well as annotate (with PFAM domains) and visualize the protein resulting from a gene fusions.
AGFusion predicts all potential fusion isoforms based on all possible isoform combinations from the gene
partners.


How can I incorporate AGFusion into a computational pipeline?

You can use the AGFusion python package, which can downloaded at https://github.com/murphycj/AGFusion.
