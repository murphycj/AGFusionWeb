What is AGFusion?
Annotate Gene Fusion (AGFusion) is a both a python package and web tool to annotate and visualize the
protein resulting from a gene fusions. The protein architecture can refer to domain annotations (from numerous
databases such as Pfam, SMART, and many more). See the figure below to view the workflow behind AGFusion.
