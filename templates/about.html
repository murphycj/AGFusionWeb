What is AGFusion?

Annotate Gene Fusion (AGFusion) and its accompanying python package predict the cDNA, CDS, and protein sequences, annotate with PFAM domains, and visualize the protein resulting from a gene fusions. AGFusion predicts all potential fusion isoforms based on all possible isoform combinations from the gene partners.



FAQ

How can I incorporate other protein domain annotations?

Currently, AGFusion only supports PFAM domains, but eventually all protein domain annotations that are available on Ensembl will be available.

How can I incorporate AGFusion into a computational pipeline?

You can use the AGFusion python package, which can downloaded at https://github.com/murphycj/AGFusion.


How do I cite AGFusion?

Manuscript under review. You can cite bioRxiv for now: http://dx.doi.org/10.1101/080903
